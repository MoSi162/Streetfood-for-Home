## Tips & Tricks

### Pro Tips
1. **Be specific** - "pad thai" finds more results than "noodles"
2. **Try variations** - Search "taco", "tacos", and "taco recipe"
3. **Browse ingredients** - Search for what you have at home
4. **Save recipes** - Screenshot the details for later
5. **Copy instructions** - Use modal for easy reference while cooking

### Accessibility
- **Tab navigation** - Navigate with keyboard
- **Enter/Space** - Open recipe details
- **Escape** - Close modal (upcoming feature)
- **Screen readers** - Full ARIA support

### Troubleshooting

**No results found?**
- Try a different search term
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a moment and search again

**Modal not opening?**
- Try clicking in the middle of the card
- Use keyboard: Tab then Enter

**Recipes look old?**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check your internet speed
